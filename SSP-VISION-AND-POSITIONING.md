## 1. What SSP Is

Select Service Pros (SSP) is a **contractor-first home services platform**, built explicitly as the alternative to Angi and Thumbtack.

We connect homeowners with vetted local contractors through AI-screened, exclusivity-capped leads — and over time, layer on warranty-backed work and financing to become a true transaction platform, not just a lead broker.

## 3. The Enemy: Why Angi and Thumbtack Are Vulnerable

The existing home services lead-gen model is broken. Both companies are losing contractor supply because:

1. **Same lead sold 5-10 times.** Contractors pay full price for a 10-20% close rate.
2. **Lead quality is garbage.** Tire kickers, fake submissions, wrong service categories.
3. **No accountability.** Contractors get charged even when homeowners ghost.
4. **Annual contracts and rising prices** with falling quality.
5. **Optimized for shareholder revenue, not contractor success.**

Contractors hate them. That hate is our wedge.

## 5. Core Differentiators (The Anti-Angi Stack)

### For Contractors
1. **Lead exclusivity cap (2-3 contractors max).** Plus an optional **Exclusive Lead tier** at premium price — one contractor, one lead, period.
2. **AI intake with full conversation visibility.** Contractors see the entire homeowner conversation, not just a name and phone number. This is a 10x quality improvement over Thumbtack's form-based leads and is already built (Anthropic-powered intake).
3. **Quality guarantee.** If a lead is fake, in the wrong service category, or unreachable after 3 documented attempts within 48 hours, it's free. No other major platform offers this.
4. **No annual contracts. Pay-per-lead only.** Cancel anytime, no clawbacks, no subscriptions.
5. **Tiered routing (Elite first → Pro → Basic).** Top contractors get a 5-minute head start on new leads.
6. **Two-way reviews.** Contractors rate homeowners (real job? paid? reasonable?). Homeowners rate contractors. Removes the asymmetry contractors hate about Angi.

### For Homeowners
1. **One vetted contractor (or up to 3), not ten cold calls.**
2. **Real conversation up front** — AI intake captures actual project details so the contractor shows up prepared.
3. **Optional warranty-backed work** (Phase 2, via SHW vendor agreement or third-party warranty partner).
4. **Optional financing on jobs over $3K** (Phase 2, via Wisetack/GreenSky-style partnership).
5. **Transparent pricing, no hidden fees.**

## 6. Phased Roadmap

### Phase 1 — Now to ~6 months: Prove the Texas Lead-Gen Model
- Texas-only operating market (Houston, Dallas, Austin, San Antonio).
- Verticals: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, general contracting.
- Goal: 50-100 active contractors and consistent paid lead flow before scaling demand.
- Differentiators in market: exclusivity cap, AI intake, quality guarantee, no contracts.

### Phase 2 — ~6 to 18 months: Become a Transaction Platform
- **Warranty-backed jobs.** White-label warranty product sold alongside the lead/job. Either through SHW (paid vendor agreement) or a third-party warranty partner. This is a moat — no other lead-gen platform offers it.
- **Financing partnerships.** Wisetack, GreenSky, or similar. Homeowner needs $8K HVAC replacement, contractor offers financing through SSP's partner. Contractor closes more jobs, SSP takes a referral fee on the loan.
- **SHW denied-claim pipeline (if/when SHW builds WF0).** Bonus lead channel, not foundation. Paid vendor relationship.

### Phase 3 — 18+ months: Multi-Metro + Contractor SaaS
- Expand to other metros (Florida, Georgia, Arizona — markets with high home-services density).
- **Contractor SaaS layer:** CRM, invoicing, scheduling tools for SSP contractors. This is the "infrastructure for contractors" play and where real enterprise value compounds. Turns SSP from a $5M lifestyle business into a $50M+ company.

## 7. Why This Won't Be Angi/Thumbtack

The rule: **never optimize for short-term revenue at the expense of contractor trust.** Angi and Thumbtack's collapse is a direct consequence of doing exactly that for a decade. We make the opposite trade.

Concrete commitments baked into the platform:
- Lead caps are platform-enforced, not aspirational.
- The quality guarantee is a published policy, not a customer service judgment call.
- No annual contracts, ever. If we change this, we lose the pitch.
- Pricing transparency for both sides.

## 8. Go-to-Market Sequence (Supply Before Demand)

Spending money on consumer marketing without contractor supply is how marketplaces burn cash and die. Order of operations:

1. **Get 50-100 Texas contractors signed up** before turning on consumer marketing.
2. **Channels for contractor recruitment:**
   - SHW's existing contractor network (ask Morris/Joey for permission to email — frame as benefit to their contractors).
   - Cold outreach: Google Maps scrape for HVAC, plumbing, electrical in TX metros. Email + SMS via A2P-registered number.
   - Facebook contractor groups (engage genuinely, don't spam).
   - Supply house partnerships (Ferguson, HD Supply, local plumbing/HVAC supply houses).
3. **Then turn on homeowner acquisition:** Google LSA, Facebook ads, SEO push using the existing 200+ keyword homepage.
