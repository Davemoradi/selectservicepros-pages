## 2. Brand Voice

- **Direct.** No fluff, no SaaS-speak. Talks like a contractor wrote it.
- **Honest about the enemy.** We name Angi and Thumbtack. We don't pretend to be everyone's friend.
- **Respectful of contractors.** They're the customer. They're skilled tradespeople running real businesses.
- **Confident, not arrogant.** We're the alternative because we built it that way, not because we're disruptors with a PowerPoint.

What the voice is **not:**
- Not "platform" or "ecosystem" or "leverage" or "synergy."
- Not soft-pedaled marketing language about "connecting" or "empowering."
- Not afraid to be specific (lead caps, dollar amounts, refund terms).

## 6. The Quality Guarantee (Published Policy)

**The SSP Quality Guarantee.** A lead is free if any of the following are true:

1. The contact information provided is fake, invalid, or non-functional.
2. The lead is in a service category the contractor doesn't offer.
3. The homeowner is unreachable after **3 documented contact attempts within 48 hours** (call, text, or email).
4. The homeowner did not actually request a service quote (form spam, accidental submission).

To claim a refund, the contractor reports the lead within 7 days through the contractor dashboard. SSP reviews within 2 business days. Refunds are issued as account credit by default, or as a refund to the original payment method on request.

We don't make you fight for this. We built the platform expecting some leads to fail — when they do, we eat the cost, not you.

## 8. Social Proof Strategy

We don't have testimonials yet. Until we do:
- **Quote anti-Angi sentiment from public sources** (Reddit, Facebook contractor groups, Google reviews of Angi/Thumbtack). Use them in ads with attribution. Don't fabricate.
- **As soon as the first 10 contractors are paying customers,** ask each for a 1-2 sentence testimonial. Use video where possible.
- **Once we have 50 contractors,** publish "the SSP contractor list" (with permission) — a public directory showing real businesses on the platform. Builds trust both sides.

---

## 9. What We Don't Say

- We don't claim to be "the best" or "#1." Empty.
- We don't promise specific lead volumes we can't guarantee.
- We don't badmouth specific contractors or homeowners.
- We don't use stock photos of fake-happy contractors. Real photos of real Texas job sites or none at all.
- We don't talk about "AI" as a buzzword — we talk about what the AI does (asks the right questions, captures the project details, hands you a usable conversation).
