Frequently asked questions

How does the tiered lead routing work?
When a homeowner submits a request, Elite members get first access for 60 minutes. If no Elite member accepts, Pro members are notified for 45 minutes. Then Basic for 30 minutes. Higher tiers always get first pick.
What is the per-lead fee?
Elite pays $15/lead, Pro pays $25/lead, Basic pays $35/lead. You're only charged when you accept a lead — pass on it and there's no charge.
Do I need a license to join?
Yes. We verify all contractor licenses through the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR) and other state boards. Only licensed, insured contractors are accepted.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
How do I get paid?
You work directly with the homeowner and collect payment however you normally would. We don't touch the transaction — we just make the connection.
What markets are currently active?
We're currently serving Houston, TX. Additional markets coming soon.
