Frequently asked questions

How does the tiered lead routing work?
When a homeowner submits a request, Elite members get first access for 60 minutes. If no Elite member accepts, Pro members are notified for 45 minutes. Then Basic for 30 minutes.
Am I charged if I pass on a lead?
No. The per-lead fee is only charged when you click Accept. Pass on as many leads as you want — no charge.
Do I need a license to join?
Yes. We verify all contractor licenses through the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR) and other state boards.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
