Getting Started

What is SelectServicePros?

SelectServicePros connects Houston homeowners with licensed, background-checked home service professionals. Whether you need HVAC repair, plumbing, electrical work, roofing, or any other home service, we match you with up to 3 qualified contractors who compete for your business — so you get the best price and service.

How do I request a quote?

Just visit our homepage and tell us what you need. Our smart intake asks a few quick questions about your project, then matches you with pros in your area. The whole process takes about 90 seconds.

What services do you cover?

We cover all major home services in the Houston metro area: HVAC (heating & cooling), plumbing, electrical, roofing, handyman services, appliance repair, remodeling & construction, painting, pool & spa, and windows & doors. If you don't see your service listed, describe your project and we'll find the right pro.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the greater Houston, TX metro area, including surrounding suburbs. Enter your ZIP code when you submit a request, and we'll match you with pros who work in your neighborhood.
Matching & Quotes

How many quotes will I receive?

You'll be matched with up to 3 licensed contractors. Unlike other platforms that send your info to 5–8 companies, we keep it exclusive so you get quality attention from each pro — not a flood of phone calls.

How fast will contractors respond?

Our average first response time is under 47 minutes. For emergency services (AC down in summer, burst pipes, etc.), many contractors respond within 15–30 minutes. You'll receive contact info and can communicate directly with each pro.

Am I obligated to hire someone?

Absolutely not. Getting quotes through SelectServicePros is 100% free with zero obligation. Compare the quotes, ask questions, and hire only if you're satisfied with the price and the pro.

What information do contractors receive about me?

Contractors receive your name, phone number, email, ZIP code, service category, and details about your project from the intake questions. They do not receive your home address until you choose to share it directly.
Trust & Safety

Are the contractors licensed and insured?

Yes. Every contractor on our platform must provide proof of active licensing and insurance. For trades that require it in Texas (HVAC, electrical, plumbing), we verify TDLR license status. We also require general liability insurance for all pros in our network.

Are contractors background-checked?

Yes. All contractors in our network undergo background screening before being approved. We take your safety seriously — only professionals who pass our vetting process can receive leads through our platform.

What if I get a bad lead or fake contractor?

Contact us immediately at info@selectservicepros.com. We investigate all complaints and will remove any contractor that doesn't meet our standards. Your trust is the foundation of our business.
Pricing

Does it cost anything to get quotes?

No — it's completely free for homeowners. There are no fees, no hidden charges, and no obligation. Contractors pay to be part of our network, which is how we keep the service free for you.

Will I get spammed with calls?

No. Unlike other lead platforms that sell your info to 5–8 companies, we limit your lead to a maximum of 2–3 contractors. You won't get phone-bombed. If you ever feel contacted too aggressively, let us know and we'll intervene.
Support

How do I contact SelectServicePros?

Email us at info@selectservicepros.com. We typically respond within a few hours during business hours (Mon–Fri, 8am–6pm CT).

Can I leave a review for a contractor?

Yes — after your project is completed, you'll have the opportunity to rate and review the contractor. Your feedback helps other homeowners and helps us maintain quality in our network.
