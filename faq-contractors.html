Joining the Network

What is SelectServicePros?

SelectServicePros is a Houston-based home services marketplace that connects homeowners with licensed, vetted contractors. We generate qualified leads from homeowners searching for home services and route them to contractors in our network based on service category, ZIP code, and membership tier.

How do I sign up?

Visit our contractor signup page. The process takes about 5 minutes: choose your membership tier, complete payment, fill in your profile and service details, then submit for review. We'll verify your credentials and activate your account within 1–2 business days.

What are the requirements to join?

To join SelectServicePros you need:
  • Active business license or contractor license (TDLR for HVAC, electrical, plumbing in Texas)
  • General liability insurance (minimum $500K coverage)
  • Ability to pass a background check
  • Service area within the Houston metro

What service categories can I sign up for?

We currently support: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, handyman, appliance repair, remodeling/construction, painting, pool & spa, and windows & doors. You can sign up for multiple categories if you're licensed for them.
Leads & Routing

How do leads work?

When a homeowner submits a service request matching your category and ZIP code, you'll receive a notification with the lead details. You can accept or pass on each lead. Accepted leads include the homeowner's contact info so you can reach out directly with a quote.

How is lead routing prioritized?

Leads are routed based on your membership tier. Elite members get first access with a 60-minute exclusive window. If they pass or don't respond, the lead goes to Pro members (45-minute window), then Basic members (30-minute window). Higher tiers = first pick of the best leads.

Are leads exclusive?

Each lead is sent to a maximum of 2–3 contractors. This is far more exclusive than platforms like Angi (which sells leads to 5–8 companies). Fewer contractors per lead means less competition and higher close rates for you.

What if I get a bad or fake lead?

We offer lead credits for verified bad leads (wrong number, fake info, duplicate, outside service area). Report the lead within 48 hours and we'll review it. If confirmed, you'll receive a credit toward your next lead. We actively filter for lead quality on the intake side to minimize bad leads.

Can I choose which ZIP codes I receive leads from?

Yes. During signup you specify your service ZIP codes. You'll only receive leads from homeowners in those areas. You can update your service area anytime through your contractor dashboard.

Can I change my tier later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your tier at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties or fees for changing.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No. All membership tiers are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with no early termination fees. We believe in earning your business every month.
Billing & Payments

How does billing work?

You pay a monthly membership fee for your tier. On top of that, there's a per-lead fee charged only when you accept a lead. You're never charged for leads you pass on. Payments are processed securely through Stripe.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) through Stripe. Payments are billed monthly on the anniversary of your signup date.

How do I cancel my membership?

Email us at info@selectservicepros.com or cancel through your contractor dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — no early termination fees, no hassle.
Support

How do I contact support?

Email us at info@selectservicepros.com. Contractor support is available Mon–Fri, 8am–6pm CT. We typically respond within a few hours.

How is SelectServicePros different from Angi or Thumbtack?

Three key differences:
  • Exclusive leads: We send each lead to 2–3 contractors max, vs. 5–8 on other platforms. Less competition = higher close rates.
  • No phone bombing: Homeowners don't get overwhelmed with calls, which means they're more engaged when you reach out.
  • Full transparency: You see the complete intake conversation before accepting a lead, so you know exactly what the homeowner needs.
