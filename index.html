Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about finding and hiring a pro.

How much does it cost to use Select Service Pros?
▾
It's completely free for homeowners. You'll never pay a fee to get matched with contractors or receive quotes. The contractors pay a small fee to receive your lead.
How quickly will I hear from a contractor?
▾
Most homeowners receive their first response within an hour. For emergency requests, contractors are often in touch within minutes. You'll receive up to 3 quotes from different pros.
Are the contractors licensed?
▾
Yes. Every contractor on our platform is verified through the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR) and is required to carry active insurance. We run background checks before any pro can join the network.
How many contractors will contact me?
▾
We match you with up to 3 qualified contractors for your specific job. Unlike other platforms that send your request to dozens of pros, we keep it limited so each contractor gives you their best attention and pricing.
What if I'm not happy with the quotes I receive?
▾
You're under no obligation to hire any of the contractors who contact you. Compare quotes, ask questions, and only move forward when you're comfortable. There's never any pressure or commitment.
What areas do you serve?
▾
We're currently serving the greater Houston, TX metro area. We're expanding to additional cities soon. Enter your ZIP code to check availability in your area.
